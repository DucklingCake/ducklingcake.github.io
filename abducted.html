Abducted

Welcome to Abducted, a puzzle game.

You are walking down the street, when suddenly a shadow appears overhead. You start too look up, but your vision goes dark.

You wake up at what seems like a spaceship, and you feel like it has been a pretty long time. There is a window to your right, and you can tell that you are defenitly in space.

With not much of a choice you must make your way out of the large spaceship.
